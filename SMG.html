Spanish Matching Game

Drag the cards to match the Spanish words with their English pictures!

The snapping into place feature makes the image transform to the size of the droppable area.

If you click the button again it will shuffle the pieces.

A success message will appear with all pieces correctly inputted.

The timer stops when the pieces are put into a slot.

Once pieces are slotted, they are not movable again, even if they are in the wrong slot.

Hovering over the slots changes the color of the slots.
